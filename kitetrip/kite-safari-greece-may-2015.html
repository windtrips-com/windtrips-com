Kite-safari in Greece May 2015

Windtrips team decicated to provide to you the best kite safaris in Greece gives you the opportunity to participate in one  of the scheduled road kite trips for summer 2015

Kite-Safari 9-16 May  2015

May is the month of thermal winds here in Greece. So actually we are going to chase nice thermals around the Greek mainland. The beaches are not crowded and if we find strong winds -instead of thermals- we are going to do some downwinds according to your kite level. At Saturday 9th of May we are going to meet up at the International Airport of Athens / International Airport of Araxos or Aktion and we going to leave immediately for our first stop near Patra at our Kite-base.Lots of different spots around is going to keep us busy for a couple of days. Then according to the forecast we are going to travel north or south to find sandy beaches , flat spots, more downwinds and some waves if we are lucky. During the week we are going to kite our ‘brains out’ to many different locations, have a glimpse to the amazing Greek scenery, we are going to eat to traditional taverns and taste the greek cuisine, meet the local and kite with them to their secret spots away from the crowds. We can actually do a lot of other stuff like mountain downhill, SUP or inflatable speed boats upon request. The kite guided is going to help you with anything you need (including kiteboarding), but for proper lessons you will have an extra charge. If you don’t have equipment or you want to rent something just send us an email.
Some of the spots we are going to visit is Drepano, Lefkada (st.John , St.nikolas), Elafonissos, Pilos, Nafplio and many more according to the wind. The last day the 16th of May we are going to head back to athens to catch your flight or continue your vacations in Greek Islands.

The price for this week is 1.290 Euro/Person for 4 people (1.050 Euro/Person for 6 people)

Price Includes :

  • Transfer to and from the airport
  • Accomodation
  • Breakfast / Dinner
  • Car hire
  • Gasoline / Tolls
  • Kite Guide / Driver
  • Test new equipment 2015 (Cabrinha / North)

Price not include :

  • Nightlife -drinks
  • Shopping

For larger or smaller groups send us an email and we will be more than happy to make a custom kite trip for you@!. Lessons can be provided upon request with extra charge.
