Kite trips in Greece & Greek Islands

Windtrips organizes Greek Seas Cruises since 2008 and specializes particularly in kitesurfing & kiteboarding-themed cruises in Greek Seas with modern sailing boats.

Employing expert crews and locally sourced associates, Windtrips guarantees outstanding holidays for kitesurfing in Greece’s clear blue waters. Greek Islands with their spectacular spots are ideally suited for kitesurfing holidays and Windtrips is the expert in this field. Actually Windtrips is the sole provider of kitesurfing-themed cruises in Greece. The Islands of Mykonos, Paros, Naxos, Rhodes, Kos, Lefkas and Corfu are destinations coveted by the world’s savviest kitesurfers. But the little-known spots we have identified on numerous sail excursions will keep pleasantly surprising you, combining ideal wind and natural wonders.
